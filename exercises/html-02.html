Team Fight Tactics (TFT)

ABOUT

What is Team Fight Tactics (TFT)? Teamfight Tactics is a strategic free-for-all game of attrition featuring iconic champions from League of Legends. Your goal? Grow your team and protect your board to become the last player standing.
